Nutella and Peanut Butter Sandwich Recipe

A very easy food to make in a hurry (I used to be obsessed with these)

Ingredients
  • Bread of choice
  • Nutella
  • Peanut Butter
Steps
  1. Toast bread
  2. Spread nutella on one side of one piece of bread
  3. Spread peanut butter on one side of other piece of bread
  4. Put pieces together
