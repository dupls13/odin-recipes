S'mores Recipe

One of the best desserts ever created, using minimal ingredients, s'mores make camping bearable"

Ingredients
  • Graham Crackers
  • Marshmellows
  • Chocolate
Steps
  1. Heat marshmellow over open fire
  2. Prep graham crackers by splitting in half
  3. Add chocolate to one piece of cracker
  4. Place cooked marshmellow in between crackers and chocolate
